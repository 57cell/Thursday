Review for Applebees

John Wilkes Booth

I had VERY high expectations for this restaurant. I was told by my entire group of friends and family that this was the best place that they have ever eaten at. They were WRONG. I had ordered the beef tartare and escargot, and when the waiter brought out my plate, he was very angry at me. He yelled over and over saying things like "stop crying" "you do not need to drink anymore orange juice" and "You can't sit in this 10 seat booth when you're all by yourself, stop spilling the orange juice everywhere" I was then kicked out of the restaurant, covered head to toe in orange juice, and had too walk the 19 miles back home. Please do NOT go here.

2/5 stars
